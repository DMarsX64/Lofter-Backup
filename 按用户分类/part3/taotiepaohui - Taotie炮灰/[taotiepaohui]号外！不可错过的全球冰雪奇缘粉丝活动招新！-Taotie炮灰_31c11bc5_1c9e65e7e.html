号外！不可错过的全球冰粉活动招新！



Discord全球最大冰粉社区Arendelle Kingdom最近启动的新项目开始招新啦！

Arendelle Odyssey（以下简称AO），是一个大型纪实活动，意在通过视频、绘画等多种媒体形式从多个角度记录并呈现自冰雪奇缘2上映后全球各大冰粉团体对这一史诗现象级作品的热烈响应，收录大量硬核独家粉丝作品，带领冰粉领略冰雪带给世界的巨大影响。这不仅是一项团队协作，更是一次难得的国际交流机会。无论你是同人老手还是cosplay大神，是剪辑大触还是绘画大师，在AO你都有施展自身才华的一片天地，让你的作品被来自其他地区的冰粉看见。尽快加入项目服务器了解详情！

❄️❄️❄️项目服语言频道概况❄️❄️❄️ 

为促进各国冰粉互通互联，此服目前设有英语、西班牙语、中文、法语区频道以及一个综合聊天频道，由“大使代表”协调管理（Nation Ambassador，目前中文区的代表有）。来自不同语言地区的冰粉可以选择在自己的语言频道用母语畅所欲言，抑或在综合频道用英语和平交流想法，分享你所在地区有关冰雪或冰雪团体活动的见闻，并获得来自其他语言地区的感动。 


❄️❄️❄️作品创作须知❄️❄️❄️ 

有意加入Arendelle Odyssey项目具体作品创作（如视频、绘画作品）的新人需要先加入讨论社区（邀链如下），填写意愿报名表并告知项目高层你的才干。随后进入项目服，加入自己感兴趣领域的创作频道（如剪辑、绘画、同人等）与其他志同道合之人组建团队以参与细分的项目计划。届时会有专人引导入列，详情请参照插图说明。所有参与人员要求在创作频道内全英文交流，英语水平不限。 


欢迎围观！创作频道组设有投稿频道，超多独家美图福利等你来，图楼们可以在做好来源出处标注的情况下任意收割，更有剪辑大触在线切磋。有意向参与项目建设的可以往投稿频道投稿你自豪的独创作品，同时欢迎转载！

❄️❄️❄️ARENDELLE ODYSSEY - TEAM GUIDE❄️❄️❄️



Hello travelers! At long last, all the Team Supervisors are here! We’re 13 and we accidentally made our own Organization XIII. Tag yourself, I’m Xigbar. So, what are we gonna be doing? Here’s a description of what each team will have to do, to give you an idea of the work your team (and you!) will have to tackle. Keep in mind a few things: -This can change. No, in fact, this will change. -There will probably be updates. Pay attention to this text. -This text talks about the overall role of the contributors, but the specific tasks will be given once we start producing the episodes. Still, you must read this to have an idea of what you’ll be doing.>>Team – Fan ArtThis is arguably the most visible part of the fan works. Over the years, fans have created hundreds of thousands of works featuring the characters and/or environments of Frozen. In AO, we want to reflect that. That’s why we’re gathering a team of fan artists to contribute to the project. The categories of fanart we will be including are the following: -Drawings, illustrations -Animations -Animatics -Comics -3D artWhat will my role be?Creating fan art. Plain and simple. We are probably gonna display pre-AO fanart too. What the specific task will be depends on the episode we’re working on, so stay tuned! 


❄️>>Team – WritingWriting is also a huge part of each fandom. There are 11k Frozen stories published in Fanfiction.net alone. While ‘fanfics’ are definitely what comes to mind when one thinks about writing, it’s definitely not all. All the categories in writing are: -Fanfics (be it multichapter, one-shots, drabbles, both canon, AU and crossover as long as it’s Frozen) -Analyses and essays -Articles -Writing projects -BooksWhat will my role be?The role of a writer in AO will be a bit more complicated. It’s not like we can display a fanfic, for instance, as easily and quickly as we can display a drawing. Therefore, it won’t be nearly as straightforward as Fan Art. But all in all, we could say writers will have mainly two tasks. First off, writers will be researching. We know a lot of you are seasoned fanfic writers or dedicated analysts who know lots about the fandom; that’s what we need. You guys know AO3 or fanfiction.net like the palm of your hand. You’re gonna be documenting what the fandom has done in terms of writing and processing that information so that it can be included in narrated form in the AO documentary. Second off, writers will be helping with the script. While we Story Trust members will be coming up with the main ideas and drafts, at the end of the day, most of us aren’t exactly writers. We will give you a guideline to work with. Sometimes you’ll have to follow this guideline strictly; sometimes you’ll have looser guidelines; and sometimes we will come up with a few ideas or vague concepts and you’ll be the ones that are gonna be brainstorming with us. Yeah, being a writer at AO is hard. 


❄️>>Team – Editing Yet another super popular medium. Editing lets fans express their creativity in a variety of ways, using the original material in new ways. These are the categories: -Image edits -Video edits (i.e. AMVs) -GifsWhat will my role be?As an editor, you’ll be producing edits for AO. Again, we might use previous works not made specifically for AO, and show off for instance some cool AMVs our members have made in the past.

 

❄️>>Team - Cosplay The name speaks for itself – this team is for all things cosplay. Be it AU or canon, as long as it’s Frozen related, it’s suitable. What will my role be?Well, showing your cosplay photos or videos, and contacting other cosplayers who’d like to participate in this project. It’d also be really interesting if each cosplayer presents themselves – who they are and why they do what they do. In which way they connect with the characters they cosplay as. That sort of thing, which will make for some really interesting discussions. Lastly, giving an insight into the process of making the costumes would be great! 


❄️>>Team - Music Frozen is a Disney Musical and its songs and score have touched the hearts of many and inspired people to produce their own covers of songs, or new compositions altogether. The categories are: -Covers (singing) -Covers (instrumental) -Original compositions inspired by FrozenWhat will my role be?AO is a documentary that would be super boring without music. You’re gonna be creating music for AO! Congrats, that’s gotta be pretty cool. As well as original works made specifically for AO, we might be showing off pre-AO stuff too. Again, the tasks will be defined in detail once we start the production of an episode.


 ❄️>>Team - Events and Miscellaneous If our plans go as according, this team will be working, among other things, on a system that we still haven’t fully implemented. Sorry about that. More information coming really soon! We're really proud of what we're planning...



最后，非常欢迎您的加入！
