【翻译填词】只因生命中第一次

其实我大学的时候曾经按照词格翻译过冰雪奇缘第一部的部分歌词2333

贴上来存个档。

Anna:

You don't have to protect me，I'm not afraid！

你不必再保护我，我并不畏惧！

Please don't shut me out again

请别再拒我于千里外，

Please don't slam the door

请别再次紧闭门扉，

You don't have to keep your distance anymore

也不必再疏远至亲与挚爱——

Cause for the first time in forever

只因生命中第一次

I finally understand

我终于能明白

For the first time in forever

只因生命里第一次

We can fix this hand in hand

能携手共度否泰

We can head down this mountain together

我们能翻越这山隘

You don't have to live in fear

驱散恐惧阴霾

Cause for the first time in forever

只因生命中第一次

I will be right here

我绝不会离开

Elsa:Anna...

Please go back home,your life awaits

请回家吧，生活在等待

Go enjoy the sun and open up the gates.

去享受暖阳，将殿门敞开

Anna:

Yeah, but……

是，但是……

Elsa:

I know...

我明白

You mean well, but leave me be

你的爱，请留我徘徊

Yes, I'm alone, but I'm alone and free

我虽孤单，但却活得自由自在

Just stay away and you'll be safe from me

请远离我，才可避免受伤害

Anna:

Actually we're not!

其实避无可避！

Elsa:

What do you mean you're not?

什么避无可避？

Anna:

I get the feeling you don't know!

我猜你不知情！

Elsa:

What do I not know?

什么我不知情？

Anna:

Arendelle's in deep, deep, deep, deep snow

艾伦黛尔已然被风雪掩埋

Elsa:What?!

什么？

Anna:

You've kind of set off an eternal winter，everywhere.

你似乎把所有地方都变成了永恒的冬天……

Elsa:Everywhere?

所有地方？

Anna:It's okay, you can just unfreeze it.

没关系，你能解冻它！

Elsa:

No, I can't, I - I don't know how!

不，我不能，我……我不知道怎样解冻！

Anna:Sure you can! I know you can!

你当然能，我知道你能！

Anna:Cause for the first time in forever

只因生命中第一次

Elsa:I'm such a fool,I can't be free

我真愚钝，无法逃离

Anna:You don't have to be afraid

你已不必再恐惧

Elsa:No escape from the storm inside of me!

漫天风暴已冻结我的心

Anna:We can work this out together

我们能并肩面对困境

Elsa:I can't control the curse

无法控制祸因

Anna:We'll reverse the storm you've made

我们能逆转结局

Elsa:Anna, please, you'll only make it worse!

安娜你让一切更无力！

Anna:Don't panic

别兢惧

Elsa:There's so much fear!

处处皆恐惧！

Anna:We'll make the sun shine bright

阳光会重耀大地

Elsa:You're not safe here

这里伏藏危机

Anna:We can face this thing together

我们能面对这一切

Elsa:No!

不——

Anna:We can change this winter weather

我们能消弭这寒季

And everything will be all right...

万物终将回归轨迹……

Elsa:I CAN'T！

不可以！
