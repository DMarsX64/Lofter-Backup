Memory Pieces

Cold.
Terrible chilliness was from the other side of the closed door.

"Elsa, I know you are in there, playing behind the door.

Please don't leave me alone right here.

'Cause it is nearly new year.

Santa Claus is coming to knock your door.

If nobody anwsers, I will...I will play with that lovely, magical reindeer! "

8-year-old Anna stood in front of the closed chilly door, talking to her chilly elder sister.

"Okay, Elsa, I wanna go! I have to go! You go play by yourself!"

Her nanny called her over for little princess's brunch.

"Elsa! I am going to eat that fantastic SNOWMAN ICECREAM!! "

"Do you want to eat a snowman or just build one?"

Little princess's voice was like a nightingale singing through the night.

Yet without receving any reply.
